This file is for listing what all the different metadata 
do depending on the block they are associated with:

BlockLog:
    0 - log end is upright
    1 - log end is pointing towards the x axis (EAST-WEST)
    2 - log end is pointing towards the z axis (NORTH-SOUTH)

BlockDoor:
    0, 1, 2, 3 - NORTH/SOUTH/EAST/WEST Closed Bottom
    4, 5, 6, 7 - NORTH/SOUTH/EAST/WEST Open Bottom
    8, 9, 10, 11 - NORTH/SOUTH/EAST/WEST Closed Top
    12, 13, 14, 15 - NORTH/SOUTH/EAST/WEST Open Top
